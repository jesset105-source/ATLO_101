What Structures & Mechanisms Do

The structures subsystem provides the physical framework that holds every component in place and survives the mechanical loads of launch. Mechanisms are the moving parts -- deployables, release devices, and drive systems that must work exactly once or repeatedly, exactly on command.

What ATLO Engineers Verify

ATLO verifies deployment mechanisms extend and lock correctly, hold-down releases fire on command, inhibit switches engage and disengage at the correct points, and that fasteners are torqued to specification with torque seal applied and documented.

KEY POINT

A mechanism that fails silently during ground testing is far better than one that fails on orbit. ATLO tests mechanisms repeatedly and deliberately, because a deployment mechanism only gets one real chance to work in space.
