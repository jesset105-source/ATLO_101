What RF/Comms Does

Uplink -- commands sent from the ground station to the spacecraft. Downlink -- telemetry and data transmitted from the spacecraft to the ground.

The combination is called the TT&C link -- Tracking, Telemetry, and Command. Verifying this link end-to-end is one of the most critical tests in ATLO.

A Word of Honesty

RF testing involves multiple pieces of equipment on both spacecraft and ground sides, all of which must be configured correctly simultaneously for the link to close. If you find yourself confused the first time, you are not alone -- ask questions before you run the procedure so you can recognize when something is wrong during it.

KEY POINT

ATLO must verify that the spacecraft cannot transmit without an explicit command to do so -- preventing unintended transmissions. This inhibit logic is a critical safety verification in every RF test.
