What Propulsion Does

The propulsion subsystem generates thrust -- a force that changes the spacecraft's velocity or attitude. On orbit, thrust is used for orbital maneuvers, station-keeping, collision avoidance, and deorbit at end of life.

Hazardous Operations -- Monopropellant Hydrazine

When actual propellant is loaded -- typically monopropellant hydrazine -- operations become among the most hazardous in the campaign. Hydrazine is toxic and volatile, requiring restricted facility access, full personal protective equipment, and strict command sequencing before any valve operation.

KEY POINT

As a new ATLO engineer, you will observe propellant loading operations before you participate in them. The safety protocols exist for good reason -- no procedure step is worth skipping when hydrazine is involved.
